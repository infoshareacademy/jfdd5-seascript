App features explained

Tell us what you need

Just give us all the inputs! First of all, we want to know what you are looking for. We can find the best matches, only if you describe the features you search for: location, age, price range, type of water sport or leisure.

The list of offers

In the next step, we will give you a list of best matches basing on your preferences. Take a look on the list, choose a few of our propositions and to compare them basing on you search criteria.

Ranking basing on your choices

Then, we will show you a ranking with a few of propositions and you will be able to decide where to go! We also give you info about weather basing on chosen spot and date. Moreover, you can check who else had similar rankings.

Doubts? Contact the providers directly

We support you with the contact data of the providers listed on our search engine. Thanks to that you can quickly obtain all the extra info you need or simply make a reservation.
